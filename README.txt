Here are the controls for the game:

Player 1:
- W, A, S, D, to move around
- Q to place bombs

Player 2:
- T, F, G, H, to move around
- R to place bombs

Player 3
- I, J, K, L, to move around
- U to place bombs

Player 4
- UP, LEFT, DOWN, RIGHT, to move around
- RIGHT SHIFT to place bombs

Power Ups:
- The red bombs gives you 5 super bombs that explode into a bigger fire
- The coins gives you a speed boost for 5 seconds
